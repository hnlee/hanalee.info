Seed-planting protocol

  1. Put seeds in 0.1% agarose.
  2. Water the surface of the soil.
  3. Use Pasteur pipette to position seeds (~ 5 seeds/well).
  4. Put in dark cold room to stratify.

0.1% agarose

  1. Combine:
    • 0.2 g agarose
    • 200 mL ddH2O
  2. Heat until dissolved.

Notes
